System "Statistics of road accident" - project of students of Software Engineering Department of Mathematics and Mechanics Faculty St. Petersburg State University.
Purpose of the project - to acquaint the user with the statistics of road accidents in the Russian regions, provide an opportunity for its visualization and comparison with statistical indicators road accidents in Finland.
On this site you can get acquainted with the road accident statistics taken from Site Road Police of Russia and site statistics Finland in a graphical and tabular format.
Here are ways to visualize the description and links to pages with the corresponding functionality.

Comparison of road accident statistics of the Russian Federation and Finland

A distinctive feature of this set of columnar, line and point graphs is the ability to compare relative death rates, the number of deaths and injuries in road accidents in the regions of Russia and Finland with an indication of the annual period.

Go to the construction

Graph statistics of road accidents with the trend line

This set of columnar, line and point graphs showing changes in the dynamics of such annual statistical indicators in the Russian regions as the number of road accidents, deaths and injuries with scaling by population and number of vehicles, as well as indicating the reasons for the road accident.

Go to the construction

Sankey chart of reasons of road accidents

This diagram represents one of the types of flow diagrams, in which riason of road accident serve as a flow, and the line width of the observed flux depends on the kinds of statistics for this reason. Parameters to visualisation the chart are the annual periods, regions of the Russian Federation and observed kind of statistics (number of victims, the number of injuries, the death toll, the number of road accidents).

Go to the construction

Pie chart reasons of road accidents

This diagram shows the percentages between the various riasons of road accidents in the Russian regions with the ability to specify the annual period and observed kinds of statistics (number of victims, the number of injuries, the death toll, the number of road accidents).

Go to the construction

Bubble chart

This chart shows the ratio between the number of road accidents, number of victims and the population and represents a two-dimensional graph where the horizontal axis is the number of accidents on the scale of 10,000 units of vehicles on the ordinate axis - the number of victims per 100,000 people in the region, and the circle with the name of region serve as a point on the graph, radius of circle is proportional to the population of the region. Circles have a different color marking statistics for different years. The parameters of the imaging digrams are annual periods and regions of the Russian Federation.
