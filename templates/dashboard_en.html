{{ title }}

"{{ title }}" system is a project of Software Engineering department students of Mathematics and Mechanics Faculty, St. Petersburg State University.
The purpose of the project is to acquaint the user with road accidents statistics in the regions of the Russian Federation and to provide an opportunity for its visualization and comparison with road accidents statistical indicators of Finland.
On this site you can get acquainted with the road accident statistics taken from Russian traffic police site and Finland statistics site in a graphical and tabular format.
Methods for visualization with descriptions and references to pages with corresponding functionality are presented below.

Comparison of road accident statistics of the Russian Federation and Finland

A distinctive feature of this set of columnar, line and point graphs is the ability to compare relative death rates, the number of deaths and injuries in road accidents in the regions of Russian Federation and Finland with specification of year period.

Go to construction

Graph of road accidents statistics with a trend line

This set of columnar, line and point graphs shows changes of such year statistical indicators as the number of road accidents, deaths and injuries in the regions of Russian Federation with scaling by population and number of vehicles, as well as indicating reasons of road accident.

Go to construction

Sankey chart of road accident reasons

This diagram represents one of the types of flow diagrams, in which the reason of road accident serves as a flow, and the line width of the observed flow depends on the kinds of statistics for this reason. Year periods, regions of the Russian Federation and observed kind of statistics (number of victims, the number of injuries, the death toll, the number of road accidents) are parameters for the visualization of this chart.

Go to construction

Pie chart of road accident reasons

This diagram shows the percentages between various reasons of road accidents in the regions of Russian Federation with ability to specify the year period and observed kinds of statistics (number of victims, the number of injuries, the death toll, the number of road accidents).

Go to construction

Bubble chart or relative road accident statistics

This chart shows the ratio between the number of road accidents, number of victims and the population and represents a two-dimensional graph where the horizontal axis shows the number of accidents on the scale of 10,000 units of vehicles, the ordinate axis shows the number of victims per 100,000 people in the region, and the circle with the name of region serves as a point on the graph, where circle radius is proportional to the population of the region. Circles have a different colors and mark statistics of different years. Year periods and regions of the Russian Federation are parameters for the visualization of this chart.
